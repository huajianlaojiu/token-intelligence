Token Flow & Economics

Understanding how tokens move through the global AI supply chain -- ratios, deflation, geography, and consumption velocity.

Input-to-Output Ratio by Region

Output tokens cost 3.8x more than input on average globally. China has the flattest ratio (~2.5x) due to competitive pricing; Europe at 4.7x with enterprise-focused models.

Price Deflation Trend

LLM token prices are declining at ~60% annually across all tiers. GPT-4-level quality cost $60/M in early 2023; equivalent quality now costs under $3/M.

Geographic Inference Flow

~42% of global AI inference in US, 28% in China, 12% in EU. China-based providers growing fastest at 180% YoY inference volume.

Consumption Velocity

Enterprise AI token consumption is growing at 340% YoY. The median SaaS product now processes 12M tokens/day in AI workloads.
